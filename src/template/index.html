Welcome to Warnati

WARNATI is a network of professionals in the fields of translation and interpretation in West. WARNATI offers complete translation and consultancy and localization services from English French into Arabic as well as from Arabic into English and French.

About Us

Membership of this Network shall remain open to any West African Citizen with solid knowledge of Arabic, English and French who is a holder of a degree, diploma or certificate in the fields of translation and interpretation and who has a reasonable number of years of practical experience in these fields at the national and international levels. It will work also with interested Arabic scholars in Africa and elsewhere . Members of Association of Conference Translators and Interpreters (AITC) and International Association of Conference Interpreters (AIIC) with Arabic as one of their major working languages shall also qualify for membership.sensisitization among scholars, irrespective of their religious or ethnic differences and backgrounds, on the importance of Arabic as one of the African languages on the continent.

Our Aims & Objectives

  • Advocacy and sensisitization among scholars, irrespective of their religious or ethnic differences and backgrounds, on the importance of Arabic as one of the African languages on the continent.
  • Promotion of good quality professional translation and interpretation.
  • Bringing together of graduates in the fields of the Arts of Translation and Interpretation in the West African Sub-Region.
  • Translation of conference documents from English and French into Arabic and vice versa and Provision of interpretation services from and into the above languages.
  • Organisation and servicing of meetings in conjunction with the existing associations, establishments, institutions and centers of translators and interpreters in the sub-region.
  • Translation of basic literature and books from Arabic into English and French for the benefit of primary pupils, secondary and college students, as well as the reading public.
  • Prepare syllabuses and manuals for Arabic studies.
  • Collaborate with WAEC, and work closely with ECOWAS, International and Regional Institutions, as well as educational and public establishments in matters relating to the Arabic language.
  • Translation of archival materials and manuscripts available at West African Universities and Institutions of learning , and in the hands of Imams and Sheikhs.
  • Translation of scripts and footnotes of films produced in West Africa for the benefit of the Arabic public ,in close collaboration with film industries in the sub-region.

Admission

Admission shall be subject to submission of a CV and recommendation from relevant institutions, and conference organizations.

Our Services

WARNATI is dedicated to servicing meetings of the Economic Community of West African States (ECOWAS), the African Union (AU),the Economic Commission for Africa (ECA), the Organization of the Islamic Conference (OIC), Islamic Development Bank (IDB) and other similar institutions in the sub-region. WARNATI shall open coordination centers in a number of West African capitals where its Members will coordinate the aims and objectives, act as interfaces between clients, institutions and translators interpreters working on projects. It will actively cooperate with institutions and professionals in the fields of Arabic education, translation and interpretation. It shall accept, through the coordination centers, documents to be processed online and duly deliver them anywhere.

Conditions of Service

The contract between WARNATI and its clients, with respect to the provision of translation, interpretation, localization and consultancy services, shall be governed by the terms outlined under conditions for services.
